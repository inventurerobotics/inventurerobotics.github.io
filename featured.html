KALIPSO ROBOTICS

Kalipso Robotics is a student-led community team from Palo Alto competing in FIRST Tech Challenge.
Founded in June of 2023, this season is the team's first.
With the support of Inventure Robotics, this rookie team has been working hard during the summer to train ourselves for the upcoming game, starting from scratch and continuously building their team and their skills.
